Technology Portfolio Management (TPM)

Although the terms "Technology Portfolio Management" and "Information Technology Portfolio Management" (ITPM) are clearly very similar, there are subtle distinctions between these two disciplines.

While ITPM focuses on the overall management of IT assets, TPM specifically addresses the strategic alignment and optimization of technology investments. Both disciplines are essential for effective IT governance and management, but TPM provides a more strategic and value-focused perspective
